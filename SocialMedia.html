Social Media Section of Personal Security

Security in social media has been a major topic recently. It is important to keep one's social media account safe as it could contain personal information or leak private information. One way to keep a social media account safe it to make a secure password. The “strength of password authentication relies on strength of the passwords”; therefore, it is important to make a strong unique password for each social media account (Ma 1). A benefit to that would be if one account gets compromised, hacked, than the rest would not be compromised. Just having a good password vastly improves personal security with social media. Another way to improve personal security is to restrict who can access your social media pages. If your page is not public than it is less known to the public and less likely to be targeted for an attack. Someone’s identity can also be stolen online just by social media. Uploading pictures or posting personal information can possibly give out enough information for some theft to steal someone's identity with. Making a strong password and watching who can see someone's social media page can prevent accounts from being compromised.
